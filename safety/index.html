Safety

A critical piece of Classic Freight Systems foundation is safety; it is considered one of the most important aspects of our company. Our safety program is continually transforming to improve and proactively identify areas of risk.

The National Safety Code (NSC) for commercial vehicles consists of 16 individual safety standards designed to reduce the number and severity of collisions involving commercial vehicles. The carrier profile includes a history of convictions, inspections and collisions provided by the Department of Transportation, law enforcement and other government agencies.

Month by month Classic Freight Systems score continues to decrease as a result of internal improvements in all areas of safety. This would not be possible without total participation from our owners, managers and all individual employees. Our fleet has been come more attractive in customer yards by showing our carefully practiced procedures, more blue stickers displayed in each driver’s window from D.O.T and incident accident rates continue to fall.

Safety Management:

  • All tractors satellites are equipped and monitored for speed, road, driver behaviors, etc.;
  • All tractors are road governed at 105 km/h;
  • Extensive orientation program for new drivers, including hands on load securement training (recognized by Department of Labour as “best in class”);
  • Ongoing training & development of current drivers with bi-annual driver safety meetings, in-house training programs for load securement, tarping, etc.;
  • Carrier Profile at Level 1, with constant focus to maintain and improve; and
  • Preparing for government audit to become Safety Certified!

Our Driver Scorecard program focuses on key performance indicators and critical event reporting that adjust employee behaviours with Classic Freight Systems strategic plan and in identifying potential trends.

We are very pleased with how far our safety has come and eager to continuously improve!
